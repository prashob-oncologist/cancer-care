Head & Neck Oncology Surgeries

Expert Surgical Care for Lip, Tongue, Buccal Mucosa, and Neck Cancers

Each procedure is performed with precision to ensure complete tumor removal while preserving vital functions and appearance. His patient-centric approach focuses on early recovery, functional outcomes, and improving quality of life.

Thyroid and Voice Box Cancer Surgeries

Expertise in Total Thyroidectomy and Laryngectomy with Voice Rehabilitation (TEP)

Dr. Prashob Kumar offers advanced surgical solutions for thyroid and laryngeal cancers, including total thyroidectomy and total laryngectomy with tracheoesophageal puncture (TEP) for voice restoration. These procedures are performed with utmost precision to ensure effective cancer removal while maximizing post-operative recovery, safety, and quality of life. Specialized rehabilitation support is provided to help patients regain their ability to speak and live confidently.

Every surgical plan is tailored to the stage and spread of disease, ensuring comprehensive and compassionate care.
